About Bookaly Woodart

Founded by Henry Taperaneyi Mapuranga, Bookaly Woodart is a Limpopo-based furniture company dedicated to creating exceptional handmade pieces. We combine traditional woodworking techniques with modern design aesthetics.

Every item is crafted with locally sourced materials, ensuring durability and unique character. From elegant bedroom suites to comfortable lounge sets, we bring your furniture dreams to life.
